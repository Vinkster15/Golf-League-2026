How the Vinksters Golf League works · 2026 edition

🏌️
League Format
How it works

The Vinksters Golf League runs alongside the PGA Tour season. Each week a new PGA Tour event is contested, and players submit a DraftKings fantasy golf lineup for that event. Your DraftKings score each week becomes your FedEx Points for that event, and scores accumulate over the course of the season just like the real FedEx Cup.

The league features 53 players competing across a full PGA Tour season, from the early season invitationals all the way through the FedEx Cup Playoffs. The season concludes at the Tour Championship in late August.

Quick summary: Enter your DK golf lineup each week → accumulate FedEx Points → top 32 make the playoffs → top 20 reach BMW Championship → top 10 compete at the Tour Championship.
📊
Weekly Scoring
DraftKings fantasy golf

Each week you enter a DraftKings fantasy golf contest for that week's PGA Tour event. Your DraftKings score for that contest is your weekly contribution to your FedEx Points total.

DraftKings fantasy golf scoring is based on real-world golfer performance over all four rounds of the tournament. Key scoring categories include:

Scoring ActionDK Points
Eagle+8
Birdie+3
Par+0.5
Bogey−0.5
Double Bogey or worse−1
Hole-in-One+5 bonus
Streak Bonus (3+ birdies/eagles)+3
Bogey-Free Round+3
Made Cut (makes weekend)+3 per round

Your FedEx Points for the week = your total DraftKings fantasy score for that event, added directly to your season total.

⭐
Bonus Points
Weekly rewards

Bonus Points are awarded on top of your weekly DraftKings score for exceptional performance. These bonus points stack with your regular FedEx Points and can make a significant difference in the standings.

AchievementBonus Pts

Note: Bonus point rules may be updated by the commissioner. The Bonus Pts column in the standings reflects your total accumulated bonus points for the season.
⚠️
Missed Weeks & Last Place
Penalties

Missing a week means you do not submit a DraftKings lineup for that event. Missed weeks are tracked in the standings and may affect your FedEx Points total depending on the league's penalty rules.

The Last Place column tracks how many times a player has finished last (or near last) in the weekly DK scoring. This is also tracked for informational purposes and may carry point implications.

⚠ Missed Week Warning: Players with high missed week counts risk falling in the standings due to lost FedEx Point opportunities. Stay consistent to stay competitive.
🏆
FedEx Cup Playoffs
End-of-season format

The playoff structure mirrors the real PGA Tour FedEx Cup format. At the end of the regular season, standings are finalized and players advance based on their cumulative FedEx Points total.

Tier Cutoff Event Location
🏆 Tour Championship Top 10 Tour Championship East Lake GC · Atlanta, GA
🥈 BMW Championship Top 20 BMW Championship Castle Pines GC · CO
🎯 FedEx Playoffs Top 32 FedEx St. Jude TPC Southwind · Memphis, TN
⛔ Eliminated Rank 33–53 Season ends —

During the playoff events, players continue to submit DraftKings lineups. Only players who qualify for each tier are eligible to compete in those events. The Tour Championship determines the overall season champion.

Every position matters: The difference between #10 and #11 is the Tour Championship. Keep grinding — standings can change dramatically in the final regular season events.
⚙️
Commissioner Notes
Questions & updates

The Vinksters Golf League is managed by the commissioner (vinkster15). Standings are updated weekly after each PGA Tour event concludes. Bonus points and any rule adjustments are at the commissioner's discretion.

If you have questions about your score, missed week status, or any discrepancies, reach out to the commissioner directly. The Google Sheet is the authoritative source for all standings data and is updated manually each week.

Good luck this season — may your drives be straight and your putts be short! ⛳
