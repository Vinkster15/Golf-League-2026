How this league works: Each week a new PGA Tour event is played. Players enter a DraftKings fantasy golf contest and accumulate FedEx Points based on their weekly DK score. Bonus points are awarded for weekly wins and top finishes. The season concludes with the FedEx Cup Playoffs — Tour Championship (top 10), BMW Championship (top 20), and FedEx Playoffs (top 32).
